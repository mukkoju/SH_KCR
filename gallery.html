CM KCR Launches ASARA Scheme

Chief Minister K Chandrasekhar Rao today asserted that the TRS Government will distribute pensions to all the eligible persons and there is no question of giving the same to those who are not eligible. All Eligible persons would get pension from today itself.

Launching the “Aasara (Help) – Pension scheme for old aged, widows and physically disabled, at Kothuru in Mahabubnagar district on the outskirts of Hyderabad, the Chief Minister said that the aim of the government is to develop the poor.

KCR aid that Rs.four crore would be allocated in the Budget every year for pensions.
